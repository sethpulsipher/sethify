## Pages

### Home `/`
- **Goal:** Establish what Sethify does, who it serves, and drive visitors to service pages or contact
- **Sections:** Hero → What We Do → Trust Block → Who We Work With → How It Works → Results/Proof → Final CTA
- **SEO role:** Broad identity terms. Passes authority to service and industry pages via internal links.

### About `/about`
- **Goal:** Build trust, humanize the studio, reinforce expertise
- **Sections:** Intro → Story → What We Believe → CTA

### Web Design `/web-design`
- **Goal:** Convert visitors searching for small business website design services
- **Sections:** Value prop → What we build → Who we build for (industry links) → Pricing → Process → CTA
- **SEO role:** Hub page for all web design industry landing pages

### Web Design Industry Pages `/web-design/[industry]`
- **Goal:** Rank for "[industry] website design" long-tail keywords, convert industry-specific visitors
- **Sections:** Industry-specific pain point → What we build for [industry] → Pricing → CTA
- **SEO role:** Long-tail keyword targets. Link up to `/web-design` parent page.
- **Verticals (Phase 2):** hvac, dental, legal, plumbing, contractors
- **Future verticals (Phase 3+):** electricians, roofing, landscaping, cleaning, chiropractic, veterinary, restaurants

### AI Systems `/ai-systems`
- **Goal:** Convert visitors searching for AI automation, AI receptionist, and AI answering services
- **Sections:** Pain point (missed calls) → AI Receptionist & Answering → Automated Scheduling & Workflows → Who we serve (industry links) → Pricing → CTA
- **SEO role:** Hub page for all AI systems industry landing pages

### AI Systems Industry Pages `/ai-systems/[industry]`
- **Goal:** Rank for "AI receptionist/answering service for [industry]" long-tail keywords
- **Sections:** Industry-specific pain point → What we build for [industry] → Pricing → CTA
- **SEO role:** Long-tail keyword targets. Link up to `/ai-systems` parent page.
- **Verticals (Phase 2):** hvac, dental, legal, plumbing, contractors
- **Future verticals (Phase 3+):** electricians, roofing, landscaping, cleaning, chiropractic, veterinary, restaurants

### Shopify `/shopify`
- **Goal:** Convert visitors looking for custom Shopify development
- **Sections:** What makes custom different → Who it's for → Pricing → CTA

### Blog `/blog`
- **Goal:** Rank for informational keywords, build topical authority, drive traffic to service pages
- **SEO role:** Targets question and cost keywords. Each post links to relevant service page. Never targets the same keywords as service pages.

### Contact `/contact`
- **Goal:** Collect inquiries via form submission
- **Sections:** Intro → Form → Confirmation message
